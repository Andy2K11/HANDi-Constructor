This documentation gives a rough guide to how the program works and gives hints to the architectural design which was intended.

While many design patterns have been used, their late adoption in the development of the program has meant that there are a few inconsistencies with their implementation.

A good place to start may be with the mvc package and also the interfaces in the network.node and network.connection packages

The non-abstract classes on the bottom level of the UML diagram are the HANDi concrete classes. In theory other types of diagram could be constructed using the same abstract classes and user interface by implementing different interfaces.
